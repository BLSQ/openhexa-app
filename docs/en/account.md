You can manage your personal information and security settings on the **Account** page in OpenHEXA.

![Account Admin Access](../assets/images/account/account.png)

## Personal information

### Update your profile

You can view and modify your account information in the **Personal Details** section:

- **First Name**: Your first name
- **Last Name**: Your last name  
- **Email**: Your email address (read-only)
- **Joined**: When you created your account (read-only)

### Save your changes

After you make changes to your personal information:

1. Click **Save** to apply your changes.
2. Click **Cancel** to discard any unsaved changes.

## Security settings

### Two-factor authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds extra security to your account. It requires a second form of verification in addition to your password.

#### Enable 2FA

1. In the **Security** section, find **Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. If it's currently disabled, click **Enable**.
3. Follow the setup instructions to configure your preferred 2FA method.
4. Complete the verification process.


!!! info "Benefits of 2FA"
    - Protects your account even if your password is compromised
    - Required for accessing sensitive features and data
    - Helps maintain compliance with security policies



!!! info "Best practices"
    - Keep your personal information up to date
    - Enable two-factor authentication for enhanced security
    - Use a strong, unique password for your OpenHEXA account
    - Regularly review your security settings
    - Choose your preferred language for the best user experience
